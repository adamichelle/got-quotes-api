The popular series Game of Thrones just ended on May 19. It's one of my favorite. Prior to the day the final season premiered I wanted to create a random quote generator of quotes from some of our favorite characters. I always wondered if there was a collection of quotes from the characters online. I found out a few websites that listed them and this API but it wasn't quite suited to my needs. I had to manually get the quotes I needed for that project. Anyways I decided to build an API for fans like myself. You can get the quotes of your favorite characters using this API.
